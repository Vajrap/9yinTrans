Neigong Grouping Tool

To use
1. Select string_buff_desc.idres or tips.idres extracted from your 9yin/res/text.package
2. Click on 'Process File' this is an extra step added since I'm too lazy to use async await, so wait for a couple seconds after clicked.
3. Click on 'Populate Group Lines' and wait a couple seconds, until it said 'Done!'
4. Click on 'Download Processed File', Boom! you got the file.
